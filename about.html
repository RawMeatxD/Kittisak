Daichi Yamamoto

Yamamoto is a bilingual rapper from Kyoto. Born to a Japanese father and Jamaican mother. He made his debut album “Andless” in 2019 which was released from the indie label Jazzy Sport . The song from the album "One Way" has been used for the game Valoroant Yoru's theme song which gave him more attention from overseas.In 2021 Daichi released a single “Kill Me,” with Chicago rapper Mick Jenkins.Which he followed with a second studio album "WHITEUBE" in June 2021.
